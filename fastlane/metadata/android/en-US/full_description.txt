An open source android app for transforming Simplified Chinese to Traditional Chinese and vice versa.

<h3>Highlights</h3>
<ol>
<li>Light weight</li>
<li>Auto detect almost everything</li>
<li>Support GBK, UTF-8, BIG5 and Unicode encoding</li>
<li>Very fast</li>
<li>Don't need internet</li>
<li>Transformation bubble, you can transform anywhere!</li>
</ol>
